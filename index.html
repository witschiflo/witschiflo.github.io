My preferences

I am currently studying computer science at the ZHAW School of Engineering.
Most of my time is spent between Zurich and Lake Constance.
Between 2014 and 2018 I completed an apprenticeship as an electronics technician (Elektroniker).
I can hardly resist making a trip to the mountains.
My first programming language was C, but now I feel comfortable in Java as well.
I love to think out of the box and taking the unconventional way.
After my apprenticeship it took me to San Diego to learn the English language.
In the summer of 2020, I received an insight into the medical device industry.
For half a year I had the opportunity to work in the development of optical distance sensors.
Artificial intelligence and big data are the topics I particularly like to engage with.
I completed my compulsory military service in the Swiss Air Force.
During my no-screen time, I like to go out and explore the area on the mountain bike.
